# Project Vision

ALADIN aims to transform the European textile and apparel industry by
enabling **local-for-local, circular and digital manufacturing
systems**.

The project combines:

-   **Digital platform technologies**
-   **Networked microfactories**
-   **Circular material innovations**
-   **Digital product passport integration**

This approach reduces:

-   long supply chains
-   overproduction
-   textile waste
-   environmental impact

The goal is to create **economically viable regional textile production
ecosystems across Europe.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Innovation

ALADIN integrates two main components:

## 1. Digital Platform

The platform will provide:

-   garment design tools
-   product customisation
-   production orchestration
-   Digital Product Passport (DPP)
-   collaboration between microfactories
-   digital supply chain integration

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Networked Microfactory System

A distributed manufacturing model enabling:

-   regional production
-   on-demand manufacturing
-   circular textile workflows
-   flexible small-batch production

This system leverages **existing manufacturing infrastructure across
Europe**.
